Define Your Audience

Who is this for? A prompt for "investors" is vastly different from one for "teenagers". This is the most crucial step for tailoring AI output.

Set the Right Tone

Should the output be "formal," "witty," "enthusiastic," or "data-driven"? Tone dictates the entire personality of the response.

Specify the Format

Don't just ask for "text." Ask for a "bulleted list," "a 5-part email sequence," "a JSON object," "a single paragraph summary," or "a 3-column table." Be specific!
